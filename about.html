Why we do what we do

Our Mission

  • At Aberystwyth Beach Clean (known as the ABC's locally!), we're dedicated to protecting the natural beauty of our coastline and ensuring it remains safe for both wildlife and visitors. Every piece of litter we remove is one less hazard for seabirds, marine life, and other animals that might accidentally ingest or become entangled in it. Not forgetting the little humans who come to visit our beach in the glorious Welsh summer!

Why we need your help!

  • Our efforts are entirely volunteer-driven, and we rely on generous support from people like you. Donations help us to:

    • Provide equipment: Gloves, litter pickers, bags, and safety gear are essential for effective cleanups.

    • Support volunteers: Many of our volunteers dedicate their time for free, but providing food, drinks and meals during events helps us keep the team motivated.

    • Expand our reach: With more funds, we can organise more events, increase awareness campaigns, and tackle other nearby areas that need attention.

  • Every penny goes toward creating a greener and cleaner Aberystwyth

Why you should join us

  • Make a difference: Be part of a movement that truly impacts the environment. Each clean-up reduces waste and creates a safer, more beautiful beach.

  • Connect with like-minded people: Joining the 'ABCs' is a chance to meet others who share your passion for nature and community. Many of our volunteers form lasting friendships through this shared cause.

  • Learn and grow: By participating, you'll gain a deeper understanding of environmental challenges and the satisfaction of making tangible progress toward solving them. Whatever your reason for joining, we'd love to have you with us!

  • Remember: If you're unable to join us for the beach cleans for any reason, please consider supporting our cause with a donation to keep this essential work going.
